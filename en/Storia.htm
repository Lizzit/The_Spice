The Spice: History

There was a spice factory on the site previously. Once the business went bankrupt, the facility was abandoned for a long time until we purchased and renovated it. To honor
the old business we chose to call the restaurant "The Spice". The city has a thriving economy thanks to tourism, in fact people from all over the world come for the beauty of its coasts.
